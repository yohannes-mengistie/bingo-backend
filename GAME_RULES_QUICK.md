# 🎯 Bingo Quick Rules

## How to Play

### 1️⃣ Choose Your Game
- **REGULAR**: 10 bet | **VIP**: 50 bet
- VIP = higher bet, bigger prize! 🎉

### 2️⃣ Join & Select Card
- Join any available game
- Select card ID (1-100)
- **Bet is deducted immediately**
- Multiple players can choose same card ✅

### 3️⃣ Wait for Game to Start
- Need **2+ players** to start
- **60-second countdown** begins when 2nd player joins
- You can still join during countdown
- **Full refund** if you leave during countdown

### 4️⃣ Watch & Mark Numbers
- Numbers drawn **every 1 second**
- Mark matching numbers on your 5×5 card
- Center cell is **FREE** (always marked)

### 5️⃣ Claim BINGO When Ready
- Complete any **row, column, diagonal, or four corners**
- Claim immediately with your marked positions (0-24)
- ✅ **Valid = WIN!** Prize added to wallet
- ❌ **Invalid = ELIMINATED** (no refund)

## 💰 Prize Calculation

**Prize Pool = (Bet × Players) × 80%**

Example: VIP (50 bet) with 10 players
- Total: 500
- Prize: 400 (winner gets all!)

## ⚠️ Important Rules

✅ **DO:**
- Claim BINGO when you complete a pattern
- Leave during WAITING/COUNTDOWN for refund
- Watch games before joining

❌ **DON'T:**

- Claim with wrong positions
